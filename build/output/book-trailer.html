Frequentky Asked Question

What if I don’t like the service, or it doesn’t give the required outcome?

If you’re unsatisfied with our service for any reason, you can take advantage of our ‘100% money-back guarantee’ and get a full refund.

Can I get revisions for my project?

Yes, you get unlimited revisions for any service in all of the packages. It is advisable to pick a few drafts you liked the most and revise them to get quality outcome.

Why should I choose LogoInUSA?

Fair question. We provide you with a team of hand-picked professionals specific to your industry. Our services are aimed to ensure customer satisfaction. We effectively respond to customer requests and efficiently meet their requirements.

How long will my project take?

It depends on the functionalities and length of your project, although regular delivery time is within 2-4 weeks. For specific deadlines, you can send us your requirements.

What are the Payment Methods?

We provide secure checkouts for Credit Card payments. You can communicate with us regarding your preference and we will do our best to fulfill it if possible.

What does the process look like?

We have a simple process that gives you the chance to provide feedback and request revisions. You can take a look at our page ‘How It Works’ [link] for more details.

What if I want help after the delivery of the project?

We provide Post-Sales support that can assist you regarding any future complications.

Can I cancel my order?

Once you approve the requirement specifications and make the payment, it is not possible to cancel your order. Our professionals will ensure that you get the services of the highest standards. Moreover, we are always open to feedback and are willing to make revisions in the project at your request.
